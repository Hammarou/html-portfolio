The Best Movies According to Emeka

My favorite movies of all-time.

The Godfather, part I

This classic movie considered (adapted from Mario Puzo's novel of the same name; and directed by Francis Ford Coppola) arguably one the greatest movies ever created.

The Godfather, part II

One of the few sequels that is considered an even better film than it's ground breaking predecessor. Which is practically unheard of in cinematic history.

Lord of The Rings: The Fellowship of the Ring

The first movie in the cinematic trilogy of J. R. R. Tolkien's epic novel--adapted by Peter Jackson--is still considered a ground breaking masterpiece, which stuck faithfully to the source material.

Lord of The Rings: The Two Towers

The second movie in Peter Jackson's fantasy adventure movie's faithful rendition of the work by J. R. R. Tolkien.

Lord of The Rings: The Return of the King

The third, and final movie in the Lord of the Ring's trilogy. As with it's two predecessors it sticks closely with the book material and beautifully directed.
